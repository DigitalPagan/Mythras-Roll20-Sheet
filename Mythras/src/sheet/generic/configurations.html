Campaign Settings

Campaign settings are generally set by your GM and should not be adjusted on a sheet by sheet basis. They are provided here for visibility, experimentation and other rare use cases. Make sure to check with your GM before making any alterations.

Game Setting

Sets the default sheet options and mechanics to match the rules as presented in the setting's source book. Individual options may be overridden below.

Magic
