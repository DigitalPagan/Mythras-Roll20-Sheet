This is the help section. It can be hidden or revealed at any time by clicking the question mark symbol on the right of the options bar above. Similiar symbols will appear throughout to share more detailed information if needed.

The sheet operates in one of two modes; edit or play. Each mode will reveal parts of the sheet relevant to the current mode. The current mode is shown above in the options bar. Clicking the current mode will toggle to the other.

    • Use edit mode when building and improving your character. Anything labeled Base Mod or Other are provided to make adjustments to the values which are considered normal. For example, to show the effects of ageing on a characteristic, add an Action Point from a gift or improvements from experience and training to a skill.
      Use play mode when actively playing the game. Anything labeled Temp reflects adjustments from in game effects. Buffs and penalties from magic for example.
    •
